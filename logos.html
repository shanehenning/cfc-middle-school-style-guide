Second Step Logo: Correct Usage

These are the approved versions of the Second Step logo. Early Learning and K–5 taglines use Frutiger Light font. Masthead uses Proxima Nova Bold. We use rich black when rendering logos for better printing results.

Plain Logo

The logo can stand alone without any taglines.

Reversed Out

The white logo can be used on a dark background when there is at least a 50% value difference between the logo and background.

Second Step Logo Incorrect Usage

The following treatments should never be used on the Second Step logo, nor should any added effects. Added effects include drop shadows, beveling, embossing, outer glow, textures, etc.

Do Not Add Effects

Do Not Change Tagline Color

Do Not Distort

Do Not Rotate

Do Not Place over Busy Background

Do Not Change Tagline

Do Not Outline

Do Not Invert Incorrectly
